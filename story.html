  Why this dataset?


When we were analyzing datasets, we were intrigued with the mobile phone service usage and the amount of money people pay toward this service. This became a point of discussion   because the internet service was bad in India and we were thinking about the days when the text service was such a prominent way. Compared to the average income,   the proportion of money spent for usage seemed excessive. The country's telecom industry was part of a larger monopoly.
